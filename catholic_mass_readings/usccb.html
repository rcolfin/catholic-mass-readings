    usccb¶

    class catholic_mass_readings.usccb.USCCB¶

    Interface for querying the Daily Readings from https://bible.usccb.org/bible/readings/

    DEFAULT_MASS_TYPES: Final[list[models.MassType]] = [DAY, YEARA, YEARB, YEARC, DEFAULT]¶
